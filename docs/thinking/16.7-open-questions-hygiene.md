### Q70 entry body explains

EXIF stripping deferred per ADR-0017 D-B + D-H. User-uploaded
photos may embed GPS coordinates, camera serial numbers,
datetime metadata, other PII. Vercel Blob CDN serves the avatar
publicly; embedded EXIF is reachable to any downloader.

Phase-17+ resolution options:
- **`sharp` server-side pipeline** (lean): well-known; ~30 KB.
- **External transcoding service** (Vercel Image Optimization /
  Cloudinary): heavier.
- **Client-side stripping pre-upload**: ~10 KB on `/profile`
  client bundle; couples to existing client component.

Couples to Q68 expansion (content moderation on uploaded
images) — both sit in the "uploaded image needs server-side
processing" category; pipeline ADR may land them together.
