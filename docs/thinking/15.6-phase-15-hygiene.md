### Architectural firsts in Phase 15

- **First user-controlled writes surface for `users` table**.
  Phase-9 established auth-side writes via Auth.js v5
  `events.linkAccount`; Phase 15 adds user-controlled writes as a
  distinct concern. Pattern establishes inheritance shape for
  Phase-16+ identity-edit surfaces (image override; opt-out toggle;
  markdown bio).
- **Second ALTER migration in project history**. First was Phase-12
  `0003_rating_challenge_review`. ADR-0014 D-E pattern crystallized
  at second exercise without surprise (no FK edge case; clean
  nullable text ALTER).
- **First sibling-helper-in-`lib/auth/`** (`getUserMetadataById`
  alongside `getLoginById`). Pattern preserves backward compat for
  existing callers while extending capability.
- **First search-param-driven success / error banner**. Phase-11's
  `?challenge=submitted` was single-state; Phase-15's
  `?saved=1 | ?error=<encoded>` is bi-state (success vs error
  pathway).
